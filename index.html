Tower Climber

Venture high into the cursed tower of Dragroth.
An ancient power has slept in this tower for centuries.
Many adventures have tried to climb it, but none have returned.
It has been said that the power that lurks there bends them
to its will and corrupts them into guarding its tower.
Are you brave enough to climb the tower or will become another lost soul...

Enter the Tower

Controls:

A - Block.
D - Attack.
Arrow Keys - Move.
F - Special Attack.
P - Fullscreen.
Esc - Exit Fullscreen.
